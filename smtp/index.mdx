## Overview

Ruach Email Delivery is built for organizations that send high volumes of email over SMTP - transactional notifications, system alerts, and customer-facing campaigns - with infrastructure tuned for deliverability and operational control.

Connect from your applications, services, and mail clients using standard SMTP. Configuration and administration are available in the **Ruach Control Panel**.

### What is an SMTP server?

SMTP (Simple Mail Transfer Protocol) moves email between mail servers. Sending large volumes from a generic mailbox often leads to throttling, poor deliverability, or spam classification.

### Service advantages

Ruach Email Delivery is designed for operational clarity and sender reputation at scale:

- Each organization's sending reputation is isolated from other tenants without requiring a dedicated IP.
- Dedicated sending reputation and suppression lists per account.
- Sending pools aligned to your traffic profile.
- Multiple accounts for separate brands or environments, managed from the Control Panel.
- **Transactional credentials** - Use SMTP credentials issued for transactional sending **only** for transactional mail (for example order confirmations, password resets, and system alerts). Sending marketing or bulk promotional mail through those credentials can damage reputation signals and **deliverability**. Use credentials and paths intended for promotional or campaign mail when the Control Panel separates them.

### Why enterprises choose Ruach Email Delivery

- **Pools and reputation** - Sending pools matched to workload; reputation separated from other customers.
- **Deliverability** - Built for strong inbox placement while discouraging abuse and supporting compliant, quality sends.
- **Support** - The Ruach team can assist with deliverability and integration questions.
- **Reliability** - We operate the sending platform and focus on consistent quality at high volume.
- **Control Panel** - Manage accounts, authorised sending domains, reputation, and suppression lists.

## Who this is for

Ruach Email Delivery fits **enterprise and mid-market teams** that need a dependable outbound SMTP path integrated into internal systems, SaaS products, and customer-facing applications.

Typical use cases include:

- **Transactional mail** - Order and shipment updates, password resets, security alerts, and account notifications from core business systems.
- **Internal and operational email** - Billing, ERP, ITSM, monitoring, and workflow tools that relay through SMTP.
- **B2B and regulated environments** - Organizations that need predictable delivery behaviour, audit-friendly configuration, and clear separation of brands or business units.
- **Marketing and lifecycle** - Newsletters and campaigns sent from your own applications, with governance aligned to your policies.
