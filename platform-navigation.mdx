## Overview

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to navigate our SMS platform dashboard. You'll learn how to access different sections, manage your campaigns, view delivery reports, and access credit balance to enhance your messaging.

### Accessing the Dashboard

1. Log in to your account at [https://accounts.netvox.ng/].
2. You'll be directed to the main dashboard.

![Dashboard Overview](images/dashboard-overview.png)

### Sending SMS

#### Step 1: Click SMS MT or Click SMS then Click Compose SMS.

![SMS MT Overview](images/send-sms-001.png)


#### Step 2: Click Campaign name to name the campaign.

![SMS Campaign](images/Click-Campaign-name-to-name-the-campaign.png)

#### Step 3: Input your SenderID.

SenderIDs are sensitive to the kind of message you want to send. Therefore use the Promotional Sender ID for Promotional content, and use Transactional Sender ID for transactional content.
![SenderIDs](images/Input-your-SenderID.png)

#### Step 3: Input your contacts.

You can input contacts manually or in bulk contacts by using group or import from a csv file or xls sheet.
![SenderIDs](images/Input-your-contacts.png)

#### Step 4: Data Coding

Always ensure you have default (GSM) selected under data coding to prevent SMS text break.
![SenderIDs](images/data-coding.png)

#### Step 5: Input your message content.
Input message content in the field and leave all other parameters as is.
![message content](images/message-content.png)

#### Step 6: Schedule your message.
Click the "Schedule" field if you want to schedule a message if not leave unselected.
![Schedule message](images/schedule-message.png)

#### Step 7: Send your message.
Click "Send" to push your messages.
![Send message](images/send-sms.png)

![Send message](images/send-sms-002.png)

### Monitoring Campaign Performance

You can see campaign statistics on the homepage of your dashboard. To view campaign reports, click "Reports"
![Campaign Statistics](images/dashboard-overview001.png)

![Campaign Statistics](images/report.png)

#### Select "List View". 

In this interface you can:
1. See the status of your messages (delivered, undelivered, submitted, failed, rejected).
2. Download delivery report.
3. Request for campaign reports for specific periods.

![Campaign Statistics](images/report02.png)


### Wallet/Credit Balance
Click the wallet icon.
![Wallet](images/Wallet.png)

![Campaign Statistics](images/contacts.png)
