## Introduction

The SMS Web GUI is the visual dashboard for managing your SMS campaigns, contacts, sender IDs, and delivery reporting without using the API. This interface is built for marketers and operations teams who want an easy way to send messages, monitor performance, and manage recipients.

### What you can do here

- Send and schedule single or bulk SMS messages
- Create and manage campaigns
- Organize contacts with groups and bulk imports
- Manage Sender IDs for promotional and transactional messages
- View credit balance and wallet activity
- Track delivery status and download reports
